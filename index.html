How do I get a .vcf file from my iPhone?

On the iPhone (no computer needed):

  1. Open the Contacts app and tap Lists (top-left).
  2. Press and hold All Contacts, then tap Export.
  3. Choose Select All Fields (keeps photos, notes, birthdays).
  4. Save to Files — then come back here and choose that file.

Alternative: on any computer, sign in at iCloud.com → Contacts, click the gear → Select All → Export vCard.

        How to import the cleaned file back into iPhone

        ⚠︎ Importing adds contacts — it doesn't replace them. If you import on top of your current contacts you'll create duplicates again.

        To fully replace your contacts:

        1. Delete the old ones first — easiest at iCloud.com → Contacts → Select All → Delete.
        2. Open the downloaded .vcf (from Files or iCloud.com → Import vCard).
        3. Confirm Add All Contacts.

        Prefer to be cautious? Keep your original export as a backup until you're happy.
